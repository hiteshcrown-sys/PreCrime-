NCIS Dashboard assets
--------------------
Place the following images in this folder (public/) for the NCIS dashboard:

1. ncis-logo.png
   - The official National Police logo (use exactly as provided; do not redraw or modify).
   - Used in the top-left header inside a circular container.

2. ncis-tricolour-bg.png
   - The faint Indian tricolour background image (use exactly as provided).
   - Used as the full-page subtle background for the NCIS dashboard.

If these files are missing, the dashboard will still load; the logo and background areas may appear blank or use fallback styling until you add the images.
